
# 📊 Excel Data Analytics & Business Intelligence Portfolio

Welcome to my Excel Portfolio! 🚀

This repository is a collection of end-to-end data analysis projects where I transform raw, fragmented data into interactive dashboards and strategic business insights. I specialize in leveraging Excel's advanced engine—**Power Query, Pivot Modeling, and Complex Formula Logic**—to solve real-world problems.

---

## 🛠️ Excel Mastery Tech Stack
* **Advanced Formulas:** XLOOKUP, INDEX/MATCH, SUMIFS, Nested IFs, TEXT logic.
* **Data Modeling:** Power Query (M), Pivot Tables, Calculated Fields, and Data Normalization.
* **Visualization:** Dynamic Dashboards, Slicers, Timelines, 3D Maps, and Pareto Charts.
* **Optimization:** Data Validation, Conditional Formatting, and Automated Reporting.

## 📂 Featured Projects

### 1. ☕ [Coffee Shop Sales & Operations](./coffee_shop_analysis/)
**Focus:** Retail Analytics & Revenue Optimization
* **Objective:** Audit daily operations to identify peak traffic hours and high-margin product bundles.
* **Key Insight:** Identified a 40% traffic surge during morning peak hours, leading to optimized staff scheduling recommendations.
* **Tools Used:** Pivot Tables, Slicers, and Trend Analysis.

### 2. ⚠️ [Road Accident Safety Report](./road_accident_report/)
**Focus:** Risk Mitigation & Geospatial Intelligence
* **Objective:** Analyze historical accident logs to pinpoint high-risk "hotspots" and environmental correlations.
* **Key Insight:** Correlated 30% of major accidents to specific low-visibility windows, identifying 5 key "Danger Zones."
* **Tools Used:** 3D Maps, Severity Profiling, and Data Sanitization.

### 3. 🇺🇸 [US Sales Market Intelligence](./us_sales_data_analysis/)
**Focus:** Regional Growth & Market Performance
* **Objective:** Evaluate sales velocity across four US geographic regions to identify underperforming territories.
* **Key Insight:** Applied the Pareto Principle (80/20 rule) to isolate the top 20% of SKUs driving 70% of national revenue.
* **Tools Used:** Time Intelligence, Regional Benchmarking, and KPI Scorecards.
