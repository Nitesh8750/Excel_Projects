
# ⚠️ Road Accident Analysis & Safety Insights

A comprehensive Excel-based diagnostic tool designed to analyze historical road accident data. This project identifies high-risk "hotspots," examines the correlation between environmental conditions and accident severity, and provides data-driven recommendations for traffic safety improvements.

---

## 🗺️ Project Roadmap

### 🔍 Phase 1: Data Sanitization & Preparation
* **Data Integrity:** Cleaned and standardized raw accident logs, addressing missing values in "Severity" and "Cause" categories.
* **Time Intelligence:** Extracted temporal features (Year, Month, Day, and Hour) to analyze peak accident windows.

### 📈 Phase 2: Diagnostic Analytics
* **Severity Profiling:** Used **Pivot Tables** to categorize accidents into Minor, Major, and Fatal to prioritize safety interventions.
* **Environmental Correlation:** Analyzed the impact of weather conditions and road surfaces on accident frequency using `COUNTIFS` and `AVERAGEIFS`.
* **Hotspot Identification:** Utilized Excel’s **3D Maps / Filled Maps** to visualize geographical clusters of frequent accidents.

### 🎨 Phase 3: Executive Dashboard Design
* **KPI Scorecard:** Developed a summary view showing Total Accidents, Fatalities, and Monthly Trends.
* **Interactivity:** Integrated **Slicers and Timelines** to allow users to filter by "Weather Condition," "Severity," and "Vehicle Type" simultaneously.
* **Risk Heatmaps:** Created visual indicators for "Danger Zones" based on the time of day and location.

---

## 🚀 Key Insights & Safety Recommendations
* **Peak Risk Windows:** Identified that **6:00 PM – 9:00 PM** accounts for 30% of total accidents, likely due to low visibility and peak commute traffic.
* **Weather Impact:** Discovered a significant spike in "Minor" accidents during rainy conditions, suggesting a need for improved road drainage or speed limit adjustments.
* **Severity Drivers:** Found that accidents involving more than 2 vehicles were 3x more likely to be classified as "Major," highlighting the risk of multi-car pileups in specific zones.
