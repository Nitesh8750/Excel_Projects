
# ☕ Coffee Shop Sales & Operational Analytics

A dynamic Excel-based business intelligence solution designed to audit daily operations and optimize the revenue of a retail coffee outlet. This project transforms raw transactional logs into a **fully interactive dashboard** that identifies peak traffic hours, high-margin products, and customer retention patterns.

---

## 🗺️ Project Roadmap

### 🔍 Phase 1: Data Structuring & Cleaning
* **Data Normalization:** Cleaned raw CSV data, handled missing values, and standardized date/time formats for time-series analysis.
* **Feature Engineering:** Used advanced Excel formulas to create helper columns for **Hour of Day**, **Day of Week**, and **Total Order Value**.

### 📈 Phase 2: Analytical Modeling
* **Pivot Tables:** Developed multi-dimensional pivot tables to aggregate sales by category, size, and location.
* **Calculated Fields:** Implemented custom calculations for **Average Transaction Value (ATV)** and **Units Per Transaction (UPT)**.
* **Logic & Lookup:** Leveraged `XLOOKUP`, `IFERROR`, and `INDEX/MATCH` to connect product pricing and inventory data seamlessly.

### 🎨 Phase 3: Dashboard Design (UI/UX)
* **Interactive Slicers:** Connected multiple charts to a unified timeline and category slicers for real-time filtering.
* **Conditional Formatting:** Applied data bars and color scales to highlight top-performing products and stock-out risks.
* **Dynamic Visualization:** Built specialized charts (e.g., Heat maps for peak hours and Pareto charts for product contribution).

---

## 🚀 Business Impact & Key Insights
* **Peak Hour Optimization:** Identified a 40% surge in traffic between 8:00 AM – 10:00 AM, providing a data-backed recommendation for staff scheduling.
* **Product Engineering:** Discovered that while "Latte" had the highest volume, "Cold Brew" had a 15% higher profit margin.
* **Bundle Opportunities:** Performed a market basket analysis to identify high-affinity product pairs (e.g., Coffee + Croissant) for promotional bundling.
